Special Education

A dedicated gateway for educators and students navigating special needs support in Ontario's schools — strategies, tools, and resources all in one place.

🌟

Ontario Teachers' Federation

Teach Special Ed

A comprehensive Ontario-based gateway developed by the Ontario Teachers' Federation (OTF) with funding from the Ontario Ministry of Education. This site provides educators with research-backed teaching strategies and resources for supporting students with special needs across elementary and secondary schools.

The resource covers a wide range of student needs — from ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder, and Learning Disabilities to anxiety management, executive functioning, and sensory integration. Every student can learn; this hub helps teachers find the right strategies to meet each learner's unique needs.
